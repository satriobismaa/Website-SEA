Cheesy Blistered Shishito Pepper Fajitas

with Lime Crema, Hot Sauce & Zesty Rice

20 min | Veggie • Seasonal

Cheesy Blistered Shishito Pepper Fajitas

with Lime Crema, Hot Sauce & Zesty Rice

Total Time
30 min
Calories:
720
Difficulty:
Medium

Descriptions

Sweet and mild shishito peppers are the star of these veggie-packed fajitas. They get a nice char with onion and scallions before being tucked into warm tortillas and topped with plenty of cheese, lime crema, and a hit of hot sauce. A side of zesty lime rice completes this easy, flavorful dinner perfect for any night of the week.

Allergens

Milk • Soy • Wheat

Produced in a facility that processes milk, eggs, fish, shellfish, tree nuts, peanuts, wheat, and soybean.

Ingredients
  • 1 unit Onion
  • 8 ounce Shishito Peppers
  • 1 unit Lime
  • 2 unit Scallions
  • 3 tbsp Sour Cream (Contains: Milk)
  • 1 tsp Hot Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Fajita Spice Blend
  • ½ cup Mexican Cheese Blend (Contains: Milk)
  • 6 unit Flour Tortillas (Contains: Soy, Wheat)
  • ½ cup White Rice

Not included in your delivery

  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cooking Oil

Fat 28g
Saturated Fat 14g

Sugar 11g
Dietary Fiber 5g
Protein 18g
